Julisa Gutierrez

Psychology

Julisa Gutierrez comes from Hesperia, California and graduated from Sultana High School as a valedictorian. At Sultana, Julisa participated in several extracurricular activities such as sports, link crew, spanish honors club, AVID, and several others. She served as the co-captain for the varsity volleyball team and president of the AVID club During her four years in high school, she attempted to make a difference in the school by choosing to be a leader.

Julisa also worked with her family’s travel management company during the pandemic, where she handled cleaning and maintenance, managed finances, and helped guests during their stay. She partnered with trusted individuals to complete these tasks in the most efficient manner. As a result of hard work, guests left five star reviews that brought attention to the company name. Julisa and her team started from scratch and eventually built a name for themselves that will carry on to their future.

Now as a student at UC Riverside, Julisa plans to pursue a major in psychology in hopes of making a difference in people’s lives, both physically and mentally. Throughout her journey in UCR, Julisa will apply for internships, maintain a sufficient grade point average, and do community service through different organizations. Once graduated from UCR, Julisa plans to attend grad school and obtain a Master’s degree in psychology.
